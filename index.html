Historical context

During the Soviet rule, half of the Eurpoe continent was separated from the rest of the world by The Iron Curtain. Every aspect of people’s lives was under the strict control of the state. Violation of privacy and freedom of expression became the new norm. Even music writing was heavily censored. Meanwhile, the United Kingdom became an example of how powerful music can be and how it brings people together.

Timeline of Events

USSR goverment clearly understood how powerful music can be and what damage it can cause to their image. By censoring music, the state took away people’s ability to express their opinion and political views in a different format as well as prevented further development of art and culture. Meanwhile, the UK’s music scene was flourishing.
Timeline provided covers political and historical contexts, the history of post-war music scenes and the use of music as a tool of expression and freedom.
